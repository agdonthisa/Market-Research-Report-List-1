The market is divided into two main types: semi-structural fillers and structural fillers. Semi-structural fillers provide thermal conductivity while allowing for some flexibility, effectively reducing thermal resistance without compromising the pack's integrity. In contrast, structural fillers offer enhanced mechanical support, maintaining the overall stability of the battery pack while efficiently managing heat transfer, making them crucial for high-performance applications.</p></p>
