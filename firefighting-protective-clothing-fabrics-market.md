<p><p>The firefighting protective clothing fabrics market comprises two main types: inherent fabrics and treated fabrics. Inherent fabrics are made from fibers that naturally resist flames and provide thermal protection, offering durability and safety without chemical treatments. Treated fabrics, on the other hand, are regular fibers that undergo chemical treatments to enhance their flame resistance and protective qualities. Both types are crucial for ensuring the safety and efficacy of firefighting gear, catering to different needs and preferences in protective apparel.</p></p>
