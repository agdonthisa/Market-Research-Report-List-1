<p><ul><li>Water Cooled</li><li>Air Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial condensing units market is primarily categorized into two types: water-cooled and air-cooled systems. Water-cooled units use water as a coolant and are often more efficient for larger installations, as they can dissipate heat more effectively. They require a water source and are typically used in large industrial applications. In contrast, air-cooled units utilize ambient air for cooling, making them simpler to install and maintain, which is ideal for smaller operations or areas with limited water supply.</p></p>
