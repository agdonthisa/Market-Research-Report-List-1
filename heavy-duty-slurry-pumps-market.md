<p><p>The heavy-duty slurry pumps market is primarily segmented into horizontal and vertical slurry pumps. Horizontal slurry pumps are designed for efficient transportation of abrasive and dense slurries, ideal for mining and mineral processing applications. They offer easy maintenance and high capacity. In contrast, vertical slurry pumps are utilized in applications with limited space and are often submerged in the slurry. They are suitable for dirty fluids and are commonly used in wastewater treatment and dredging. Both types are essential for heavy-duty industrial operations.</p></p>
