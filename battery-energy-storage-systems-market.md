<p><p>Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) encompass various market types, primarily including secondary batteries and flow batteries. Secondary batteries, such as lithium-ion and nickel-metal hydride, are rechargeable and widely used for applications like electric vehicles and grid storage. Flow batteries, on the other hand, store energy in liquid electrolytes, offering longer discharge durations and scalability, making them suitable for large-scale storage. Other market types may include technologies like supercapacitors and solid-state batteries, catering to specific needs in energy storage solutions.</p></p>
