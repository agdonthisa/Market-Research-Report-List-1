<p><p>The thermal power equipment market comprises three primary segments: boilers, steam turbines, and generators. Boilers generate steam by heating water, serving as the core component in thermal power generation. Steam turbines convert this steam's thermal energy into mechanical energy, which is vital for electricity production. Generators then transform the mechanical energy from turbines into electrical energy. Together, these components work in synergy to efficiently produce electricity in thermal power plants, catering to growing energy demands globally.</p></p>
