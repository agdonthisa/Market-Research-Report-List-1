<p><p>The liquid no-clean flux market is categorized into two main types: Low Residue and Extremely Low Residue. Low Residue flux offers a minimal amount of residue post-soldering, which can be easily left on the board without affecting performance, making it suitable for many applications. Extremely Low Residue flux, on the other hand, leaves an even smaller amount of residue, allowing for enhanced cleanliness and reliability, particularly in high-performance electronics where residue could impact functionality or performance.</p></p>
