<p><p>Textile scouring agents are essential for removing impurities from fabrics before dyeing. The market is divided into non-ionic and anionic types. Non-ionic agents, characterized by their neutral charge, are effective for delicate fabrics and offer reduced foaming, making them suitable for a variety of applications. Anionic agents, on the other hand, possess a negative charge, providing strong cleaning capabilities particularly for removing oily stains and dirt. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing fabric quality and ensuring compatibility with various dyeing processes.</p></p>
