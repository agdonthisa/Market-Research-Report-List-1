<p><strong>The Multipurpose Spray Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Hot Melt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multipurpose spray adhesive market is categorized into three types: solvent-based, water-based, and hot melt adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding properties suitable for various materials but may emit harmful fumes. Water-based adhesives are less toxic and easier to clean, making them popular for indoor applications. Hot melt adhesives are applied in a liquid state and solidify upon cooling, providing quick bonding and versatility. Each type caters to different user needs, balancing performance, safety, and ease of use.</p></p>
