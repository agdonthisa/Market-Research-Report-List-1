<p><p>Alkanolamides are nonionic surfactants derived from the reaction of fatty acids with alkanolamines, such as monoethanolamine (MEA) or diethanolamine (DEA). Alkanolamides are commonly found in shampoos, detergents, and cosmetics, where they enhance product performance and stability.</p><p>The Alkanolamide Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period.
