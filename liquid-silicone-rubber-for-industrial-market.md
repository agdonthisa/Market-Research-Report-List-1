<p><p>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) is a versatile material widely used in industrial applications. The one-component liquid rubber is pre-vulcanized, allowing for easy processing and mold filling without the need for additional mixing, making it ideal for automated production. In contrast, two-component liquid rubber requires a curing agent to achieve optimal properties, offering greater flexibility and customization in formulations. Both types exhibit excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and mechanical strength, making them suitable for a range of industries, including automotive, electronics, and medical.</p></p>
