<p><p>Integral concrete color refers to pigments or dyes that are mixed directly into the concrete during the batching process, resulting in a uniform and long-lasting coloration throughout the material. Integral concrete colors are available in a variety of shades, including earth tones, grays, and vibrant hues, catering to diverse architectural and design needs.</p><p>The Integral Concrete Color Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecast period.
