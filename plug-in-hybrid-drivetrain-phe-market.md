<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Plug-in Hybrid Drivetrain (PHE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tandem Series</li><li>Parallel Series</li><li>Tandem-parallel Series</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) employ distinct drivetrains: </p><p>1. **Tandem Series**: The electric motor powers the vehicle, while the internal combustion engine (ICE) only acts as a generator.</p><p>2. **Parallel Series**: Both the electric motor and ICE can independently drive the vehicle, optimizing efficiency based on driving conditions.</p><p>3. **Tandem-Parallel Series**: This combines both previous systems, allowing flexible operation where the electric motor and ICE can operate in tandem or individually, enhancing performance and efficiency across various scenarios. </p><p>These designs cater to diverse consumer preferences and regulatory emissions standards.</p></p>
