Fully automatic bagging machines operate independently, handling the entire packaging process with minimal human intervention, making them ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, semi-automatic bagging machines require some manual involvement, offering flexibility and cost-effectiveness for smaller operations or varying product sizes. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in packaging, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
