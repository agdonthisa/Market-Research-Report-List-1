<p><strong>The Aerospace Metal Hose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strip-wound Hoses</li><li>Corrugated Hoses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace metal hose market is primarily categorized into two types: strip-wound hoses and corrugated hoses. Strip-wound hoses are constructed by spirally winding metal strips, providing flexibility and high strength, making them suitable for applications requiring dynamic movement. In contrast, corrugated hoses feature a series of grooves or corrugations along their length, allowing for enhanced flexibility and vibration absorption. Both types are designed to withstand extreme temperatures and pressures, ensuring reliability in aerospace environments.</p></p>
