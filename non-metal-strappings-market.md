<p><p>The non-metal strappings market primarily encompasses PP (polypropylene) and PET (polyethylene terephthalate) strapping, both of which are crucial for securing and reinforcing packages. PP strapping is lightweight, flexible, and cost-effective, making it popular for lighter loads. Both types are widely used in industries such as packaging, shipping, and logistics, providing reliable solutions for securing goods during transit and storage.</p></p>
