<p><strong>The Wireless Smart Video Doorbell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Doorbell With Battery</li><li>Doorbell Without Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless smart video doorbell market is divided into two main types: battery-powered doorbells and hardwired doorbells. Battery-powered doorbells offer flexibility in installation without the need for electrical wiring, making them ideal for renters or locations without existing doorbell setups. However, they require regular battery replacements. In contrast, hardwired doorbells connect directly to an existing doorbell system for a consistent power supply, eliminating battery concerns but necessitating professional installation and potentially limiting placement options. Both types enhance home security and convenience.</p></p>
