<p><p>The Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) battery market primarily comprises two types: Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries utilize a fiberglass mat to absorb the electrolyte, preventing spills and allowing for efficient gas recombination. They offer high discharge rates and are commonly used in demanding applications. Gel batteries, on the other hand, contain a silica gel that immobilizes the electrolyte, providing excellent deep-cycle performance and vibration resistance. Both types require minimal maintenance and are favored for their safety and reliability.</p></p>
