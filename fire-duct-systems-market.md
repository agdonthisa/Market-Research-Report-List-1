<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Positive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire duct systems are essential for smoke and heat management in buildings, categorized into passive and positive market types. Passive fire duct systems rely on inherent structural materials and compartmentalization to contain smoke and flames, requiring minimal active intervention. In contrast, positive market systems employ mechanical ventilation to actively control smoke movement, enhancing safety during emergencies. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring building safety, adhering to regulations, and protecting occupants, but differ in their reliance on active versus passive strategies.</p></p>
