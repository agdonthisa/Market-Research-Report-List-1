<p><p>The corrugated polyethylene board market primarily consists of two types: general corrugated polyethylene board and functional corrugated polyethylene board. General corrugated polyethylene board is typically used for packaging, protection, and construction purposes due to its lightweight and durable nature. In contrast, functional corrugated polyethylene board is engineered for specific applications, featuring enhanced properties like moisture resistance, UV stability, or impact durability, making it suitable for demanding environments such as agriculture, transportation, and industrial uses.</p></p>
