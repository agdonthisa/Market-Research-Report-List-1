<p><p>Time Temperature Indicator (TTI) labels are critical tools for monitoring the freshness of perishable goods. The market can be divided into two main types: Color (Only) Based and Barcode Based. Color based TTIs change hue in response to temperature fluctuations, providing a visual cue of product safety. In contrast, barcode based TTIs incorporate digital elements, allowing for real-time tracking via scanners. Both types are essential for maintaining food quality and safety in the supply chain, catering to different consumer needs.</p></p>
