<p><strong>The Outdoor Gas Grills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Propane Gas</li><li>Natural Gas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outdoor gas grill market primarily comprises two types: propane gas and natural gas grills. Propane gas grills use liquefied propane stored in portable tanks, offering flexibility and mobility, making them ideal for camping or tailgating. In contrast, natural gas grills connect directly to a home’s gas line, providing an endless fuel supply and often delivering higher heating efficiency. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, with propane grills favored for portability and natural gas grills appreciated for convenience and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
